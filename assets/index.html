About

For over 8 years I have been involved closely in the fitness industry and am qualified to instruct in fitness training as a Personal Trainer. I design enjoyable individual fitness training programmes for all ages and abilities. I can also advise on nutrition and weight management. As your trainer, I aim to meet your requirements whether in your home, at your local park or workplace. I am hearing impaired and have a cochlear implant. I hear well and clients find communication fine.
